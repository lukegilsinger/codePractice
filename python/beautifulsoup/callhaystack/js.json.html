Above Allocation represent additional Sales Opportunities in case of shortage situations (usually AI allocations concerning CP line of business). The operational forecast (Best Estimate External Sales Forecast for Crop Protection) is adjusted to the allocated volumes, Best Estimate Above Allocation indicates the lost sales linked to the shortage.\n

The forecast of Above Allocation is normally done at Country level as part of the S&OP/S&OE process and can be used for prioritization in case updated supply or sales plans are allowing for additional sales. Similarly, it can be used for field sales persona to capture demands exceeding their allocated supply.\n

Above Allocation does not have a direct impact on Supply Plans or Sales Commitments, it is a reporting only information to enable quick decision making and create transparency.\n
